1. u/one_plain_slice [score=8, created=2024-05-09T14:19:29]: Heparin is used during *diagnostic* caths only when going radial in an effort to prevent radial artery occlusion. Usually no heparin is used during diagnostics via femoral. But during interventional procedures, anticoagulation is always used. Much of the specifics here are institution/operator dependent.
