## Comments
1. u/Whatifjunkie [score=2, created=2024-05-30T07:42:24]: Wall tension is a measure of the force that the walls must resist in order to generate contractions or eject blood from the ventricles during each heartbeat. Wall stress refers to the changes in pressure throughout a cardiac cycle and how this affects cardiac muscle mechanics during contractions.
