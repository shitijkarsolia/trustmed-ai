## Comments
1. u/herseyhawkins33 [score=8, created=2025-11-06T15:56:14]: The number of total units in the pen doesn't matter as long as it's the exact same medication. Read the label carefully to confirm. The only difference is you'll run out of the 100 unit pen sooner. That said, if you're still concerned go back to the pharmacy to consult with them.
2. u/mYstiSagE [score=1, created=2025-11-09T09:15:40]: Both my pens, glargine (long-acting) and humalog (fast-acting), are 100 units, the same size as my vials I used to use. the 200 units will simply last longer, per the other commenter. Also, my pens are a different color, meaning they are different insulins. Very important to read the labels and check with your pharmacist about any concerns you may have.
