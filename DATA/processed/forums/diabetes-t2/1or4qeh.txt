I saw the improvement you saw only after i started on metformin and jardiance.. So, does your treatment include any such medications? Then, what you see is the result of that medication... each acts in a certain way to either cut down glucose conversion or to expel it effectively. If you are seeing improvement after diet changes, you are not giving your body the same type and amount of food to raise sugar as much as before. If you're exercising more, you're just burning more.
