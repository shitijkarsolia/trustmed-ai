My PCP who has been treating my HBP for the past 10 years changed me over Labetalol about 5 months ago. Some historical context - I have been on a number of other HBP meds but had to stop due to reactions or no improvements. PCP sent me to a Endocrinologist who had me get a CT Scan of my Aldosterone glands to see if they were the culprit of the HBP. Scan results came back fine, but Endo said that doesn't mean anything and further testing (Salt Test) is needed to validate. Due to my already HBP the Endo didnt want me to do the Salt Test and instead jumped right to taking the Eplerenone. The Endos rational was that if I did have Aldosterone glands over producing then the combination of these drugs would see "near instantaneous" improvements in my BP. Thoughts about this approach and about taking both drugs at once?
