2. u/makergrl [score=1, created=2025-08-31T19:44:17]: I have been on so many diets and wanted something simple this time. I try to have a protein, fat, carb, and green veg with most meals. I make containers of ingredients and then fix myself a bowl at mealtime. Example meals are salmon, 1/2 cp quinoa, arugula with avocado oil and salt. Or ground beef, small sweet potato and green beans. Bfast is 2 scrambled eggs with avocado, apple slices and pnut butter. I shoot for about 2/3 cp protein, maybe 1/2 cp carb and as much green veg as I want. It is simple but works well. My fasting glucose has gone from 160 to 90 in just 3 months. I feel so much better too!
3. u/TheRealLougle [score=0, created=2025-08-30T04:51:28]: I have been on this journey to reverse my type 2 diabetes blood markers for 2.5 years and my diet has evolved greatly during this journey. I went clean keto in the beginning while I was still taking medication. Now, with no medication of any kind, I eat a zero-carb high fat and exercise daily. I am finishing up a 72-hour fast and just got back from a 10-mile bike ride. My blood sugar is currently 58 and I am feeling amazing. Explore the journey and accept that you will evolve overtime.
