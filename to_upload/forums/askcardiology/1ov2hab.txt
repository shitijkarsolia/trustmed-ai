1. u/Impressive_Donut3122 [score=4, created=2025-11-12T08:58:56]: You may have acid reflux or a heart rhythm issue triggered by certain foods; see a gastroenterologist and cardiologist. When your stomach or esophagus is irritated, it can activate the vagus nerve, which connects your gut to your heart. This can disrupt your heart’s normal electrical signals, causing faster, irregular, or weird” beats.
